Architecture

Clay + HubSpot + Slack

HubSpot is the display layer, not the engine. Graph logic runs in Clay and results are pushed into HubSpot as custom properties.

◆
Clay
Data Engine
Stores relationship tables, runs automated enrichment on a recurring schedule, computes connection paths, and pushes results to HubSpot.
⬡
HubSpot
Display Layer
Custom properties on Company and Contact records surface connection data where AEs already work. Purely additive — no architectural changes.
⌘
Slack
Access & Input
Slash command for instant lookups. Also an input channel — team members flag relationship intel that routes into Clay.
